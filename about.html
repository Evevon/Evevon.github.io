About Me

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Evelyne van Oers. Ever since I was young I had an interest in visual art and storytelling. Creating stories and art has been a hobby of mine for as long as I can remember. By the time I went to university, my interests had broadened and I decided to do a study Artificial Intelligence at the University of Amsterdam. Although I liked the coding and psychology involved with the study, I soon realized I missed a more creative, human-oriented aspect. This led to my decision to study a master UX design, also at the University of Amsterdam. Nowadays, I'm working on various projects: artistic projects such as my own webcomic, game design and game art projects, and web development projects such as my master thesis.
